Welcome to SciClaimEval

SciClaimEval is a shared task focused on scientific claim verification. Participants will evaluate systems that assess the validity of scientific claims based on scientific evidence.

This page provides information about the task, leaderboard, submission dates, and the organizing team.

How to Participate

Instructions on how to participate in SciClaimEval will be provided soon. Stay tuned for dataset access, submission guidelines, and evaluation criteria.
